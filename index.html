Emergent Media

For the most part, this project was heavily research orientated. The goal was to deliver the history of the music platform using an engaging and creative technique. My creation exploits the modern concept of updates and applies it to each major advancement in sound technology since the first 'Phonograph' in 1877. This was then tied togther using the Adobe Premier Pro editing software.

Firefly Farm

Firefly Farm is my first attempt at an immersive project. I'm using Three.js, AR.js and Tween.js as my core frameworks within JavaScript. Firefly Farm is an example of a first person shooter type platform. Instead of shooting people the goal is to kill as many fire flies as you can, however, if you miss a firefly, another is born and the game gets that little bit harder. An additional feature includes the use of a marker that once detected will give the viewer access to a firefly being born and released into the world through AR technology.

#arjs #tweenjs #threejs #augmentedreality #interactive #immersive #shoot #play #game
